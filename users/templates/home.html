The Need

Kenya's transportation sector contributes significantly to the country's growing carbon emissions, accounting for approximately 13% of total greenhouse gases and rising each year with increased vehicle ownership. Major cities like Nairobi and Mombasa face dangerous air quality levels, with pollution often exceeding WHO guidelines by 4-5 times. Our e-mobility platform addresses these challenges by leveraging Kenya's impressive renewable energy infrastructure (over 90% of electricity from clean sources) to offer a sustainable transportation alternative that reduces emissions, alleviates urban congestion, decreases dependence on imported fuels, and supports Kenya's commitment to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 32% by 2030 under the Paris Agreement.
